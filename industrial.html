Industrial & Civil Applications

Beyond defense — drones for industrial scale.

The same flight control, mesh networking, and integrated avionics — adapted for the cadence of energy, utilities, agriculture, and public-safety operators.

Tethered
Infrastructure ISR

24/7 persistent monitoring · Refineries, substations, ports, perimeters

SDR's T-dori platform repurposed for industrial infrastructure surveillance — a tethered drone holding station 100 meters above critical assets, transmitting continuous high-resolution imagery and thermal data over the same fiber-optic line that powers it. 24-hour endurance with a 25-minute untethered dash mode for inspecting adjacent assets. Deployed for refineries, transmission substations, port perimeters, and oil & gas facilities.

Smart Farm
Agri Platform

Multi-drone field operations · Spraying, sensing, surveying

3D-Printed
Distributed Manufacture

On-site airframe printing · Field-replaceable platforms

SDR's 3D-printed VT-Tail enables an entirely different industrial paradigm — airframes printed on-site at the customer location, anywhere in the world. Replacement components, mission-specific variants, and rapid-iteration prototypes produced from a single CAD library. Reduces logistics burden, accelerates field replacement, and enables operators to manufacture drones in-theater rather than waiting on global supply chains.

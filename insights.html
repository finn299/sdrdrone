Korea's $2.4B drone procurement reset.

Defense · Korea
USD 2.4BAdditional drone procurement
Defense Policy · 2025

Korean defense budget redirected from attack helicopters to drones — SDR among two firms named to presidential TFT.

Following the demonstrated tactical utility of drones in the wars in Ukraine and Iran, the Republic of Korea redirected KRW 3.3 trillion of attack-helicopter budget into drone procurement, with an additional USD 2.4B authorized. SDR was selected as one of two domestic drone companies under the Task Force for Strengthening Drone Defense Capabilities, established by presidential directive — currently serving as advisory committee member for government drone policy formulation and implementation.

Market position
70%Korean Army training drones
Defense · Standard Platform

SDR's platform is the standard training drone of the Korean armed forces.

Adopted across the Army, Navy, Air Force, Police, and Fire Department under government-approved curriculum, SDR holds 70% market share in Korean Army training drones. Over 10,000 cumulative pilot certifications and 500+ real operational missions validate the platform's flawless field record across all five service branches.

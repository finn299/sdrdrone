Leadership & Operating Team

The team building the platform.

SDR Drone Inc. is led by three principals who together carry three decades of unmanned-aviation engineering, defense procurement experience, and global commercialization expertise — supported by SUNDORI Co., Ltd., the Korean engineering and manufacturing team behind every platform.

Principals

Three principals. One platform.

Founder-engineer, international strategist, and managing partner — the leadership of SDR Drone Inc. operates as a tight, three-person command for technology, global strategy, and commercial execution.

Founder · Chief Executive

Cho Sun-sik

Founder & CEO · Platform Architect

Founder of SUNDORI Co., Ltd. and SDR Drone Inc. Three decades of unmanned-aviation engineering — from Korea's first commercial unmanned airships in 1991 through the development of the SDR Multi Flight Control system, the SDR-ONE integrated avionics board, and today's defense-standard drone platform. Architect of the company's technology roadmap and platform strategy.

International Strategy

Leo Chung

International Strategic Officer · Programs & Partnerships

Leads SDR's international strategy, defense-program engagements, and government partnerships across nine markets — including the United States, Japan, India, Indonesia, Thailand, Mongolia, Bangladesh, Cambodia, and the Republic of Korea. Responsible for procurement pathways, technology-transfer frameworks, and long-horizon market positioning.

Managing Partner

Finn Gallagher

Managing Partner · Capital, Corporate & Commercial

Managing partner of SDR Drone Inc. Leads the U.S. corporate platform — capital strategy, SPAC-track listing, U.S. defense-channel development, and operating governance. Background in cross-border industrial transactions, technology commercialization, and U.S. corporate structuring for international defense and dual-use platforms.
